Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this website is to brighten up people’s day through movie and book quotes. I want people to leave my site with a smile on their face because of the quotes that they read.

Audience

My audiences can be all ages, but I think it will be mainly of young adults and teenagers. Younger people look for funny, or inspiring quotes to lift their spirits from the hard ties in life.

Dynamic elements

I’m thinking of one of two ways, 1: With generating a random quote every time they press a button or reload the page. Or 2: Letting them choose a category that will poll quotes from.
